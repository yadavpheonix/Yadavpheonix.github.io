The Growing Crisis of E-Waste

What happens to your old phones?

Electronic waste is currently the fastest-growing domestic waste stream in the world. When electronics end up in trash dumps, dangerous chemicals like lead, mercury, and cadmium leak directly into groundwater systems.

By reusing old tech components like wires, computer keys, and casing components, we completely divert toxics away from ecosystems while creating stunning pieces of tech-art.
